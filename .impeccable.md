## Design Context

### Users
Hiring managers, engineering peers, and potential collaborators reviewing the site quickly on desktop and mobile.

### Brand Personality
Technical, confident, human.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep the current editorial/engineering look, but add balanced motion that feels deliberate rather than flashy. Prioritize clarity and momentum in the homepage reading flow.

### Design Principles
1. Guide attention with purposeful motion around the hero and section reveals.
2. Keep interactions responsive and lightweight for balanced web performance.
3. Use transform/opacity animation only; avoid layout-heavy effects.
4. Respect accessibility by disabling almost all animation under `prefers-reduced-motion`.
5. Optimize the homepage first before broadening to other pages.
